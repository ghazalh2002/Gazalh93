About Me

My journey from physics to front-end development

My Story

My journey began at Hewad School, where I first discovered my love for problem-solving. I then pursued physics at Herat University for two years, developing analytical thinking and a deep appreciation for how things work.

The transition from physics to design happened at Code to Inspire, where I graduated in graphics and motion design. This experience opened my eyes to the creative possibilities of technology and visual storytelling.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills as a front-end developer student at CTI, and I've earned certification from the University of California, Berkeley in HTML and CSS. I'm passionate about photography and proficient in Microsoft programs.
